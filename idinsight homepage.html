Sectors in which we work

Agriculture

We have deep agricultural sector experience and help clients design, target, enhance, and measure agricultural programs and services.

Education and training

We combine global evidence and local data to strengthen education and training programs with public, NGO, and low-cost private education providers.

Environment and energy

We support leaders faced with consequential questions about how to protect the planet while creating opportunities for climate-affected communities.

Financial inclusion

We bring a strong track record supporting design, targeting, and evaluation of programs that serve people who are unbanked.

Gender equity

We help partners advance gender equity through the use of data and evidence.

Governance

We work to improve public sector performance through close partnerships to address diverse problems from tax administration to training civil servants

Health and nutrition

We help clients design, target, enhance, and measure the impact of health and nutrition interventions, services, and systems.

Livelihoods and basic income

We help optimize cash transfer programs by measuring impact and using data-driven insights to improve the efficiency of targeting and transactions.

WASH

We support service providers to strengthen water, sanitation, and hygiene programs that improve peoples health and wellbeing.

Data Science solutions for social challenges

IDinsight is using AI to help social sector partners create chatbots, optimize resources, target beneficiaries and more.

Data science and engineering

Our capabilities

Impact evaluations

An impact evaluation estimates the impact of a policy, program, or intervention on one or more outcomes.

Data science and engineering

Our Data Science team builds and deploys software and AI solutions to some of the biggest challenges in the social sector.

Learning partnerships

Learning partnerships are long-term often embedded engagements to answer important questions as they arise using a broad methodological toolkit.

Monitoring systems

Monitoring systems efficiently capture reliable data, providing visualization and analysis to identify triggers for action.

Process evaluation

Process evaluations can help decision-makers understand if their program is running as expected.

Private: Preference elicitation

Preference elicitation in household surveys and lab-in-the-field experiments are designed to understand core beliefs and preferences.

Quick turnaround surveys

IDinsight recruits, trains, and supervises survey data collectors to conduct rapid telephone or in-person surveys.

Capacity building

Capacity building bolsters clients skills and knowledge to strategically use evidence and data to meet their goals.
